My Hobbies & Interests

Fishing

During the warmer months, one of my greatest passions is fishing. My favorite spot is at my family cabin, where I spend quality time fishing with family and friends. While I enjoy the thrill of catching various fish species, I'm particularly fond of largemouth bass.

Why It's Important

Fishing provides a perfect blend of relaxation and excitement, while also offering valuable time with family and friends in nature.

Hockey

Hockey has been a significant part of my life since childhood. I played competitively from 5th through 11th grade, and while I no longer play at that level, I still enjoy recreational skating and games.

Why It's Important

Hockey taught me valuable lessons about teamwork, dedication, and perseverance. It continues to be a way to stay active and connected with family, especially when skating with my brothers on our backyard rink.
