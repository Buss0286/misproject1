Discover UMD & Duluth

Why I Chose UMD

I chose the University of Minnesota Duluth because it offers the perfect balance in terms of size and location. The campus is large enough to provide numerous opportunities and resources, yet small enough to maintain a close-knit community feel. This "just right" size means I can enjoy personal attention from professors while still having access to extensive facilities and diverse academic programs.

The location was also a major factor in my decision. Duluth's unique position on Lake Superior provides an amazing backdrop for my college experience, combining urban amenities with easy access to outdoor activities.

Campus Highlights

Campus Life

Life at UMD is vibrant and engaging. One of the highlights is being able to attend UMD Bulldogs hockey games, where the energy and school spirit are incredible. Our Division I hockey program is a source of pride for the entire community, creating unforgettable experiences and bringing students together.

Academic Experience

The academic environment at UMD provides the perfect blend of challenge and support. With smaller class sizes, I've been able to develop strong relationships with my professors and peers, enhancing my learning experience in the Finance program.

Duluth Area

Duluth is a city full of character and natural beauty. The iconic Aerial Lift Bridge and Canal Park serve as perfect spots for relaxation and entertainment. The city's natural landmarks, including Park Point and the Superior Hiking Trail, offer countless opportunities for outdoor activities. The breathtaking views of Lake Superior, especially during sunrise and sunset, combined with the surrounding forests and hills, make Duluth a truly special place to live and study.
