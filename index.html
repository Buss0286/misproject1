Fishing

During the warmer months, one of my greatest passions is fishing. My favorite spot is at my family cabin, where I spend quality time fishing with family and friends. While I enjoy the thrill of catching various fish species, I'm particularly fond of largemouth bass. The peaceful moments by the water and the excitement of each catch make this hobby truly special.

Hockey

Hockey has been a significant part of my life since childhood. I played competitively from 5th through 11th grade, and while I no longer play at that level, I still enjoy recreational skating and games. Some of my favorite moments are spent skating with my brothers on our backyard rink, and I occasionally visit public rinks to keep my skills sharp. It's more than just a sport - it's a passion that brings my family together.
